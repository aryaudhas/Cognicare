How It Works

From data to
understanding

01
Continuous Activity Capture
Every reminder sent, chat message, alert, image analysis, and patient response is timestamped and logged to the patient's daily activity record.
02
Gemini Writes the Summary
At day's end, Gemini Flash reads the full activity log and writes an empathetic, clinical narrative covering cognition, mood, medication, and safety.
03
OpenRouter Fact-Checks
A second LLM independently reviews the summary against raw logs — identifying hallucinations, unsupported claims, and omissions. Accuracy is scored 0–100.
04
Caregiver Receives Verified Report
The final corrected summary — with full audit trail and error annotations — is delivered to the caregiver dashboard. Trust through transparency.

Verified Intelligence

Two minds are
better than one

Single-LLM summaries can hallucinate — inventing details that were never in the activity log. CogniCare's dual-LLM architecture puts every generated sentence through a second independent audit before it reaches a caregiver.

✓
Hallucination Detection
Facts not grounded in the activity log are flagged and removed from the final report.
✓
Omission Recovery
Important events the first model missed are surfaced and included in the corrected summary.
✓
Full Audit Trail
Caregivers see exactly what was corrected, with severity levels and specific error annotations.
